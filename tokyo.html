Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo is a very technologically advanced city so it has many tourist attractions for me to explore. First I would go to the beautiful Tokyo National Museum which was established in 1879, where I can see master pieces of art and antiquities of Japan. After I would visit the Tokyo DisneySea which is a 176 acre theme park at the Tokyo Disney Resort located in Urayasu, Chiba, Japan, just outside Tokyo. There I would have a blast going on all the rolloar coasters and rides, while also getting wet from the water rides. Next I would go to the Kabuki-za which is a principal theater in Tokyo for the traditional kabuki drama form. Lastly I would go to the Miraikan which is a National Museum of Emerging Science and Innovation which is a museum created by Japan's Science and Technology Agency.
