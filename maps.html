Paris, France

This is the Effiel Tower in Paris, France which is a popular tourist attraction. The effiel tower is an Observation tower/ Broadcasting tower which is located in the 7th arrondissement in Paris. The Effiel Tower is 984 ft tall and it has 3 floors and 8 elevators. In Paris I would also visit many other tourist attractions like the Arc de Triomphe, Louvre, and Le Marais. In Paris I would also want to visit the creepy Catacombs of Paris which are underground ossuaries in Paris, France which hold the remains of over six million people in a small part of the ancient Mines of the Paris tunnel network.
