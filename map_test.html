Dream Vacation Flight Route

My Dream vacation would be traveling to many locations, not just one location. Since I would be traveling to many locations around the world, This is the Flight route I would take. It starts from Los Angeles because I live here, then it goes straight to the touristy city of Paris, France. The flight then goes straight to the beautiful city of Stavanger, Norway. After having a blast in Norway, the flight would continue and fly across the Mediterranean sea straight to Israel aka "The Holy Land". After enjoying my time in Israel, the flight would continue flying to its last destination, the technologically advanced city of Tokyo, Japan.
