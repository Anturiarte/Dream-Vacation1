Israel

In Israel, I would travel too many cities throughout the country. First, I would visit Jerusalem which is the capital of Israel, and in Jerusalem I would visit the amazing Western wall, Tower of David, and the Jerusalam Biblical Zoo. Next I would travel to the beautiful city if Tel Aviv which is a city next to the coast. In Tel Aviv I would visit the Tel Aviv Museum of Art, Bialik House, Tel Aviv Promenade, and the Tel Aviv Port. Lastly I would visit Eilat which is a southern Israeli port and resort town on the Red Sea, near Jordan. In Eilat, I would swim in the amazing red sea and in the hotel pools.
